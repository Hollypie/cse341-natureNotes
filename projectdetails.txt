Collections:

Users

Fields:

username (string, unique)

email (string, unique)

passwordHash (string, for OAuth / bcrypt)

experienceLevel (string: beginner, intermediate, advanced)

preferredTerrain (array: trail types, e.g., forest, mountain, paved)

favoriteTrails (array of trail IDs)

createdAt (date)

Trails

Fields:

name (string)

location (string or coordinates)

type (string: hiking, cycling, multi-use)

difficulty (string: easy, moderate, hard)

length (number, miles/kilometers)

elevationGain (number)

description (string)

Gear

Fields:

name (string)

type (string: hiking, cycling, camping)

purchaseDate (date)

lastUsed (date)

condition (string: new, good, worn, broken)

ownerId (ref to Users)

notes (string)

Wildlife Sightings

Fields:

trailId (ref to Trails)

species (string)

quantity (number)

date (date)

location (string or GPS coordinates)

notes (string)

photoUrl (string)
